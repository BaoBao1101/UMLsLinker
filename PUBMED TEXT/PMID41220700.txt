We report three cases of patients who developed CAH within a timeframe of 10–14 months following spinal surgery, which was complicated by cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) leakage. The close temporal relationship and the lack of other identifiable causes in their medical histories may suggest a possible causal link. While definitive evidence directly connecting complications from lumbar surgery to the onset of CAH is lacking, the observed temporal association and absence of alternative explanations in the patients’ medical histories indicate a potential correlation.
